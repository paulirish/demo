How to manage the website

  • To name your site, have a line that says name: Your Site Name
  • To give your site a specific theme, have a line that says theme: theme-foldername
  • To give your site a specific template, have a line that says template: template-filename
  • To give your site a custom 404 page, have a line that says 404: url
  • To give your site any other properties, have a line that says anything: whatever-you-want
  • For readability sake, please use lowercase and no spaces when applicable

How to manage the sitemap

  • To give your page a specific url, add [url:the-url-you-want]
  • To give your page a specific theme, add [theme:theme-foldername]
  • To give your page a specific template, add [template:template-filename]
  • To make your page hidden in the navigation, add [hidden]
  • To give your page any other properties, add [anything:whatever-you-want]
  • To create a child page use a tab
  • Remember, child pages inherit the parent page's remaining preferences followed by the site's remaining preferences.
  • For readability sake, please use lowercase and no spaces when applicable

How to manage pages

  • To include functionality tags into your page, use a comment tag like <!-- FUNCTIONALITY --> or just ##FUNCTIONALITY##. Anything between <!-- BEGIN:FUNCTIONALITY --> and <!-- END:FUNCTIONALITY --> or ##BEGIN:FUNCTIONALITY## and ##END:FUNCTIONALITY## will be removed before display. None of this is case sensative.
  • To include a sitemap, use NAVIGATION-SITEMAP. Examples might include <!-- NAVIGATION-SITEMAP -->, ##NAVIGATION-SITEMAP##, ##BEGIN-NAVIGATION-SITEMAP##This will not be displayed.##END-NAVIGATION-SITEMAP##.
  • To include root navigation, use NAVIGATION-ROOT.
  • To include sibling navigation, use NAVIGATION-SIBLINGS.
  • To include child navigation, use NAVIGATION-CHILDREN.
  • To include the web site title, use SITE-NAME.
  • To include the page title, use NAME.
  • To include any other page attributes, use * where * represents the attribute. Examples might include ##THEME##, ##COLOR-SCHEME##.
  • To include any other site attributes, use SITE-* where * represents the attribute.
